************
Provisioning
************

There are several capabilities in the |conduit| provisioning engine. Things ranging from application / service deployments via containers, virtual machines, and even bare metal. Deployment management and app template construction are also core aspects of the provisioning engine.
Take advantage of custom tasks and workflows within any environment by building tasks and workflows from those tasks. There is a lot of information to cover with regards to provisioning but |conduit| makes it intuitive and smooth.
